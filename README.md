# #82 Focus Stone

A digital "weight" for your deep work. Unlike traditional timers that distract you with ticking seconds, Focus Stone provides a silent, steady presence to anchor your concentration.

## Core Philosophy
- **1 Tool / 1 Action**: Tap a duration, and the stone appears. That's it.
- **Zero Distraction**: No countdown numbers, no progress bars. Just the stone.
- **Digital Zen**: Inspired by the "stone-gazing" meditation, it transforms your device into a physical boundary for focus.

## Features
- **Screen Wake Lock**: Uses the Screen Wake Lock API to prevent your device from sleeping during focus sessions.
- **Battery Saver Mode**: The UI switches to a pitch-black (#000) background during operation to minimize power consumption (especially on OLED screens).
- **Anti-Burn-in Animation**: The stone features a subtle "1/f fluctuation" breathing animation to prevent display ghosting and provide a calming visual.
- **Safety Limit**: Automatically releases the screen lock after the set time (Max 60 mins) to protect your battery.

## How to Use
1. **Choose Time**: Select 15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes.
2. **Deep Work**: Once the stone appears and the screen turns black, start your task. Do not touch your device.
3. **Release**: The screen will return to white when the time is up. You can also manually interrupt by tapping "Tap to Release".

## Design System
- **Active State**: `#000000` Background / `#333333` Stone
- **Idle State**: `#ffffff` Background / `#333333` Stone
- **Border Radius**: `15px` (Mandatory for all elements)
